Aviation Investment Project Description

## Project Overview

For this project, we have implemented various techniques to clean the data, analyze it, 
and draw visualization to better understand what we're working with to enables us generate insights for 
a business stakeholder, Capital Investments.

### Business Problem

Capital Investments wishes to diversify its business portfolio, by venturing into the
aviation industry by purchasing planes for both private and commercial use. We need to determine
the viabilit of the investment and to know which crafts to get and which ones to avoid.

### The Data

We shall be using dataset that contains accident data from the National Transportation Safety Board 
expanding 1962 through to 2023 that occured in the United States and international waters.
